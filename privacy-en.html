Privacy Policy

Last updated: September 28, 2025

1. Data we collect

We collect only the data returned by TikTok API during user authorization. This includes:

  • Basic profile info (open id, display name, avatar)
  • Video metadata when users upload via our service

2. Why we collect data

We use this data to allow users to sign in and publish videos. We do not collect extra personal data beyond what the TikTok API returns.

3. Data sharing

We do not share personal user data with third parties except when required by law. We may share aggregated, non-identifying analytics with partners for product improvement.

4. Data storage and retention

We store tokens and minimal metadata required to manage publishing flows. Users can request data deletion by contacting us at mobileproleagueauto@gmail.com.

5. User rights

If you are in a jurisdiction with data protection laws (for example, GDPR or LGPD), you have the right to access, correct or request deletion of your personal data. Contact us at the email above.

6. Security

We take reasonable measures to protect user data, including encryption of tokens and restricted access. However, no system is 100% secure.
